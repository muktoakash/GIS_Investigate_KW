Analysis and results of the project.

The NDVI map was used to identify the vegetation cover in the city of Kitchener. The map shows the areas with the highest vegetation cover in green and the areas with the lowest vegetation cover in red. The map can be used to identify areas that need more vegetation cover and areas that have sufficient vegetation cover.
