Description:
Abstract:
The coverage originated from the Vector Map (VMap) Level 0 database, (third edition of the Digital Chart of the World, DCW). The second edition was a limited release item published in 1995. The product is dual named to show its lineage to the original DCW, published in 1992, while positioning the revised product within a broader family of emerging VMap products. VMap Level 0 is a comprehensive 1:1,000,000 scale vector basemap of the world. Outer frame of the current ArcView project is used to clip taled Vector Map level 0 data.
Purpose:
This data is for reference purposes. This shapefile is also used in the ArcExplorer project.
