GIS Projects

    Emergency Medical Services in the city of Kitchener

    This project studies the location of Emergency Medical Services such as hospitals, Urgent Care centers, Walk-in clinics and Emergency Ambulance services. Voronoi polygons are used to identify the regions closest to these facilities. The project also studies the distribution of these services in the KW region and identifies areas that are underserved.

    Elevation mapping of Kitchener

    This project consider elevation data for Kitchener, Ontario. A purpose of this project is to create 3D representation of the city of Kitchener. This report also looks at the cycling infrastructure in the city and how the elevation data can be used along cycling routes.
